Common Issues

Common issues with vehicle transmissions include slipping gears, delayed or hard shifting, and transmission fluid leaks. These problems can lead to reduced driving performance and potentially serious damage. Regular maintenance helps prevent these issues and ensures optimal transmission performance..

  • Slipping Gears
  • Delayed or Hard Shifting
  • Transmission Fluid Leaks
  • Unusual Noises
  • Warning Lights
  • Burning Smell
